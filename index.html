About Me

I am Dr. Ankit Raj, a physician and public health specialist with an MD in Community Medicine. My professional experience spans clinical practice, health policy research, and AI safety projects. I founded P-Value Consultancy to mentor medical researchers in low-resource settings and have published widely on public health, health financing, and digital health. I am passionate about leveraging Generative AI to improve healthcare research, policy, and communication.

About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research is an 8-week live cohort course by GRAPH Network (University of Geneva). It equips participants with no-code AI tools for automating tasks, analyzing data, and enhancing research communication.

  • Learn how LLMs work, and generate research papers, websites, and presentations.
  • Use AI for automated data analysis, dashboards, and visualizations.
  • Apply AI in literature reviews, text analysis, and knowledge synthesis.
  • Build custom GPTs and chatbots for research assistance.
  • Automate repetitive workflows and literature monitoring.
  • Create AI-driven research communication tools such as video abstracts and infographics.
  • Capstone project showcasing real-world applications of AI in research.

Features include weekly live workshops, personalized feedback, a capstone project, and lifetime access to resources.
